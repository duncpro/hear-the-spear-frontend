Privacy Policy

HearTheSpear is...

person_outline Completely Anonymous We will never ask for personally identifying information such as your name, birthday, or gender. We do request your email address but only to verify your enrollment at FSU.
replay Limited in Scope We only collect data on the music you stream most frequently. HearTheSpear can not see or modify your complete music library.
money_off Advert-free We will never share your email address or listening history with third party advertisers.

FAQ

If I use Facebook to login to Spotify, can HearTheSpear see my profile? No. Spotify does not share user’s Facebook information with third-party apps like HearTheSpear.
people Can other students see what I'm listening to? Yes! But they won’t see your name, student email address, or any other personal details. HearTheSpear is completely anonymous.

Revoke Access

If you ever decide you want to stop participating you can always revoke HearTheSpear's access to your Spotify account. See Spotify's official website.
