How it works

HearTheSpear is a project I developed during the break between summer and fall classes this year. The goal is to make a list of our school's favorite songs and artists.

HearTheSpear uses the Spotify API to track the most popular music at Florida State University. Are you an FSU student? Link your account to participate.

Privacy

To protect your privacy, HearTheSpear only collects data on your most frequently listened music. HearTheSpear can not read or make changes to your playlists, library, or preferences. You can always disconnect your Spotify account from HearTheSpear by visiting Spotify's official website.

Open Source

You can contribute to HearTheSpear's code by making a pull request at github.com/duncpro.
